Pay-as-you-go charges are applied when you exceed Local Talktime & SMS usage of your Base Plan. It also includes PAYG roaming rates. See the exact rates for each country at https://www.circles.life/sg/rates-data-roamer/
IDD charges include international Calls, SMS & MMS made and received while in Singapore.
If customer receives a call from an international/Global number while in Singapore (incoming call):
- Talktime allocation will be used up first. If there are no more talk time minutes left, customers will be charged as local voice calls where it is chargeable on a one (1) minute pulse basis for the first minute at $0.08, and on a thirty (30) seconds pulse basis at $0.04 thereafter.
- No charge if Unlimited Incoming Calls are added on the account.
Auto-Boost is automatically activated when you run out of data. These charges will be reflected in your next bill. (Only applicable for the first 25 Auto-Boosts per billing cycle. For any Auto-Boosts thereafter, it will be charged directly to your card instead)
Boosts give you additional data on top of your base plan and can be purchased anytime, like when you are running out of data. This includes Unlimited Data (valid until the end of the day), 2 GB and 1 GB (valid until the end of the month). They are charged to your card immediately upon activation and will be reflected in your next bill.

One-time charges may include registration fee, redelivery fee, number change fee, lost sim fee, and debit adjustments. They will be charged and reflected in your next bill.
Addons include Unlimited Data, Unlimited Incoming Calls, Unlimited Outgoing Calls, Unlimited XP, and Super Roam. The first time you purchase/repurchase it, it will be charged immediately to your card. Subsequently, you will get monthly recurring charges from the next month onwards.
Roaming voice services are chargeable on a one (1) minute pulse basis
Roaming MMS services are chargeable on an event basis on top of the data consumed for uploading and downloading the content
If a customer does not answer the incoming call while roaming, no roaming charges will be applied.

If you signed up for a number transfer, you will be entitled to get up to $10 waiver (which will give you $28 for 20GB / $38 for 100GB). Please note your first month will be prorated based on your activation date and your waiver will be prorated too. You will receive a full $10 waiver for the following months as they are not prorated. Upon successful delivery, your SIM card will be automatically activated with a temporary number even if the transfer is not yet started. Note that your $10 waiver will not be applied when the SIM card is activated under a temporary number. The waiver will be given only after transfer is successful. Once transfer is completed, your waiver will be applied in the next 12 months' bills.

We suspend lines on these scenarios:

- More than 1 unpaid bill
- - 2 & above
- 1 unpaid bill that has been outstanding for more than 50 days.

- (Current month’s “estimated” bill amount + Total outstanding amount) > $200

- - Customers > Details Tab > Credit Cap
- - Customers > Details Tab > Current Cycle Plan > usage panel (PAYG)
